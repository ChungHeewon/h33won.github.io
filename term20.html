Regressive Taxing

Bob makes 15$ an hour. Every year bob ends up making $3000 John makes $6000 a week, and at the end of the year he ends up making $20000. The government now charges everyone the same in the sake of fairness and takes $1000 from everyone. By doing this they take 33% of bob money but only .5% of Johns money. THis makes it harder for bob to survive but doesn't affect John as much. THis is what regressive taxing is. By taking everyone a fixed amount the richer people have a lower % of their income taken. Which makes it harder for the poor people to survive.
