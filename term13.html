Substitute Good

Jack likes to buy goods that are made in america. But lately there is a lot of inflation and his dollar is unable to buy what it used to be. He also isn't feeling as happy when he buys his made in american gummy bears so he starts to get the cheap foreign swedish fish instead. He now feels happier since he is spending less on these substitute candies because they give him the slightly lower happiness at a much lower cost.
