Supply and Demand

Pelkey owns a store. He has 100 pounds of beef jerky and he wants to have 2 chickens. Chad want to have a pound of beef jerky. He gives Pelkey 2 chickens for 100 pounds of jerky. Chad has a demand for jerky and Pelkey had a supply for jerky. Chad has a supply for chickens and Pelkey had a demand for chickens.
