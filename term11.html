Monopoly

Pelkey owns a store called Board Games inc. He likes making board games but then he becomes smart. He uses his profits to buy the people who harvest trees and sell paper. He than buys every supplier of wood in his area and starts to expand. Eventually he starts to buy the stores around him and becomes the cheapest board game seller. After all the stores go out of business he is the only board game seller. He than has an idea and makes a new board game called monopoly to show how he has total control over the prices of the field that he is in.
