Velocity of Money

Peter Pelkey owns a country. He wishes for the country to have a stronger larger economy so he decides to pump more money into the poor people. His velocity of money goes up, but so does inflation. This is because the people in his country like buying a set amount of things with more money. Because poor people need to spend money to survive, while rich people tend to save it by giving the money to the poor person the economy tends to have more transactions.
