Marginal Propensity to Save

John is a member of a household. Each time the household earns 1$ they spend a part of it and they save a part of it. John doesn't makes enough for him to spend all his money and have some left over, so he has to save this money. John saves 20 cents for every 80 he spends. His MPS is .20 because that's how much he saves over how much he earns.
