Necessity

Jill likes to go up a hill in order to get water. However she keeps falling down and cant get any water at all. However she owns a diamond mine that she gets a lot of diamonds for. Jhon can climb the hill but he likes diamonds a lot. A necessity for jill to live is water so she is willing to give a lot of diamonds to john for some water.Jhon is able to use the necessity of water to make a higher profit off of jill.
