Absolute Advantage

Pete Pelkey and Ben Recktenwald are both artists. Pete Pelkey can create 40 paintings or 20 drawings on any given day, while Ben Recktenwald can create 60 paintings or 60 drawings on any given day. Since Ben can create more paintings and drawings than Pete on any given day, Ben has an absolute advantage in the creation of paintings and drawings. If a group can create more of a certain product than any another group during a specified amount of time, then that group has an absolute advantage in the creation of that product.
