Stocks

Alphamara is the leader of a company. He wants to invest more in his technology but can't find enough money to do so. He needs money so he has an idea. He can sell a part of his idea and in return get money. This is called selling stocks. If his idea is good and viable people will buy his stock in order to get a profit.
